The Betta

Whether you know it as a betta splenden or a Chinese fighting fish, odds are, you have an idea of what a betta fish is. One of the most commonly kept fish on the planet, the betta fish is also a deceptively misunderstood pet. Though not particularly picky, they require the same tender love and care as any pet.

Food

Betta fish are voracious carnivores. Mosquito larvae and betta specific fish pellets are a good start to a betta’s diet.

Environment

Betta fish should be kept in tanks no smaller than five gallons. They like heat, plants, and hiding places.

Maintenance

Test a betta tank’s water at least once a week. Aim for a twenty five percent water change once a week.
